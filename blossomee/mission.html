Credo is a company built around care and curiosity. We offer clean beauty, vetted to the highest standard.

Our mission is to change the way people think about what goes into products, onto your skin, and ends up in the environment. The world doesn't need another kinda-checking-the-clean-box beauty retailer or product line. The industry needs a reliable voice to ask the questions that lead to more informed choices.

hey

We believe that all consumers deserve the best and safest cosmetic products. We work diligently to understand the hazards and potential risks of ingredients that we use everyday, and over a lifetime, through our own science-based hazard assessments and partnerships with experts in the industry. We recognize that the beauty industry is not fully transparent in its practices, so we work hard through our questioning, our policies, and our advocacy work to lead the change needed.

PLANET

No Blossomee products are tested on animals. but we also push beyond this with our souring guidelines. Sustainable sourcing meaning doing as little harm as possible to the environment to meet current needs, without compromising the needs of the future. We ask our brand partners to share information on ingredient/ material source, safety substantiation, ingredient purity, sustainability and material composition.
